Rights issue atau penawaran umum terbatas menjadi instrumen penting bagi perusahaan publik Indonesia untuk menggalang dana ekspansi di 2025. Tren ini didorong oleh kondisi pasar yang kondusif dan kebutuhan modal untuk pertumbuhan bisnis.

## Overview Rights Issue 2025

### Statistik YTD (Juli 2025)
- **Jumlah Rights Issue**: 18 perusahaan
- **Total Dana Terhimpun**: Rp 24,5 triliun
- **Rata-rata Size**: Rp 1,36 triliun
- **Success Rate**: 89% (fully subscribed)
- **Average Discount**: 15-20% dari harga pasar

### Perbandingan Historis
- **2023**: 22 rights issue (Rp 31,2 triliun)
- **2024**: 16 rights issue (Rp 19,8 triliun)
- **2025 Projection**: 25-28 rights issue (Rp 35-40 triliun)

## Rights Issue Mechanics

### 1. Pricing Strategy
**Discount Analysis:**
- **Average Discount**: 15-20% dari market price
- **Range**: 10-25% tergantung market conditions
- **Factors**: Volatility, liquidity, company quality

**Pricing Considerations:**
- Market price stability
- Trading volume
- Shareholder base
- Market sentiment

### 2. Subscription Patterns
**Retail vs Institutional:**
- Retail participation: 35-40%
- Institutional: 60-65%
- Foreign investors: 15-20%

**Subscription Rates:**
- Fully subscribed: 89%
- Oversubscribed: 34%
- Undersubscribed: 11%

### 3. Timeline Analysis
**Average Process Duration:**
- Planning to RUPS: 2-3 bulan
- RUPS to OJK approval: 1-2 bulan
- Approval to execution: 2-4 minggu
- Total timeline: 4-6 bulan

## Investment Strategy

### 1. Pre-Rights Analysis
**Due Diligence Framework:**
- **Financial Health**: Debt levels, cash flow, profitability
- **Use of Proceeds**: Growth potential, ROI projection
- **Management Quality**: Track record, corporate governance
- **Market Position**: Competitive advantage, industry outlook

**Valuation Metrics:**
- **P/E Ratio**: Pre vs post-rights adjustment
- **P/B Ratio**: Book value dilution impact
- **ROE Impact**: Earnings dilution vs growth
- **Dividend Yield**: Payout policy implications

### 2. Participation Decision
**Factors to Consider:**
- **Discount Level**: Immediate value creation
- **Growth Prospects**: Long-term value potential
- **Dilution Impact**: Ownership percentage change
- **Opportunity Cost**: Alternative investments

**Participation Strategies:**
- **Full Participation**: Maintain ownership percentage
- **Partial Participation**: Selective based on conviction
- **Non-Participation**: Sell rights in market
- **Arbitrage Play**: Buy rights, sell shares

### 3. Post-Rights Monitoring
**Performance Tracking:**
- **Execution of Plans**: Use of proceeds monitoring
- **Financial Metrics**: ROE, ROA, growth rates
- **Market Performance**: Share price vs benchmark
- **Dividend Policy**: Payout sustainability
