Sektor consumer goods Indonesia menunjukkan resiliensi yang kuat di 2025, didukung oleh program pemerintah yang pro-konsumsi, pemulihan daya beli masyarakat, dan adaptasi perusahaan terhadap perubahan pola konsumsi digital.

## Overview Sektor Consumer Goods

### Market Size dan Pertumbuhan
- **Total Market Size**: Rp 2.850 triliun
- **Growth Rate 2025**: 6,8% (vs 4,2% di 2024)
- **Per Capita Consumption**: Rp 10,4 juta/tahun
- **Urban vs Rural**: 65% urban, 35% rural

### Segmentasi Pasar
**Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG):**
- Personal care: 35% market share
- Food & beverages: 40%
- Home care: 15%
- Health & wellness: 10%

**Consumer Durables:**
- Electronics: 45%
- Furniture: 25%
- Automotive: 20%
- Others: 10%

## Key Growth Drivers 2025

### 1. Program Makan Bergizi Gratis
**Direct Impact:**
- Stimulus konsumsi Rp 71 triliun
- 82,9 juta beneficiaries
- Substitution effect pada pengeluaran keluarga
- Multiplier effect ke sektor terkait

**Beneficiary Companies:**
- ICBP: Processed food supplier
- INDF: Staple food provider
- CPIN: Protein source supplier
- UNVR: Indirect benefit dari increased disposable income

### 2. Demographic Dividend
**Population Dynamics:**
- Millennial & Gen Z: 60% of population
- Middle class expansion: 52% by 2025
- Urbanization rate: 58%
- Digital native consumers: 75%

**Consumption Patterns:**
- Premium product adoption
- Health & wellness focus
- Sustainability consciousness
- Digital channel preference

### 3. Digital Transformation
**E-commerce Growth:**
- Online penetration: 35% (+8% YoY)
- Social commerce expansion
- Live streaming shopping
- Direct-to-consumer models

**Omnichannel Strategy:**
- Traditional retail: 65%
- Modern retail: 25%
- E-commerce: 10%
- Integration across channels
